EPΦL — Philosophy Association

A student association at EPFL organising philosophy lectures, debates, and writing contests. Open to all students since 2021.

← Other Projects

Bringing philosophy into a scientific school.

EPΦL has been running at EPFL since 2021. The association brings professors and researchers for lectures of about an hour followed by discussion, on subjects linking science, philosophy, ethics, and consciousness. All events are free and open to the full student body.

Seventeen lectures have been held at EPFL since the association was founded. The format is a talk followed by questions and discussion. Topics have included free will, human nature, consciousness, the relationship between science and philosophy, and the place of the human in the natural world.

The association also organises writing contests in collaboration with other student societies, inviting students to submit essays and short fiction on philosophical themes.

Role: President and founder since 2021. Directing the programme, coordinating speakers, and organising events across the academic year.
