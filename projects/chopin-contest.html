Swiss Student Chopin Contest

Concours Chopin Étudiant, a Swiss intercantonal piano competition open to all university students, dedicated exclusively to Chopin repertoire. 3rd edition, 2026–2027.

← Other Projects

A competition born on campus.

The Swiss Student Chopin Contest started at EPFL and UNIL in 2024. Students playing on campus public pianos during their lunch breaks gave us the idea: they needed a proper stage, and their classmates deserved a chance to hear them. Every round of the competition and every recital throughout the year is free. We raise the funds so the door stays open for everyone.

The contest is open to all university students in Switzerland. The repertoire draws exclusively from Chopin. Each season opens with a HEMU recital series on campus and builds through semi-finals to a Grand Final. The 2026 edition raised 8,000 CHF to keep the Grand Final free, with 400 people in the room and over 2,000 students reached across the year.

The contest currently runs across two cities, Lausanne and Zürich, with plans to extend across Switzerland in the coming editions. Partners include HEMU, the Société Frédéric Chopin de Genève, ETH, and UZH.

Role: President. Founded the contest in 2024 and oversees the overall direction, programme, sponsorship, and organisation of each edition.
