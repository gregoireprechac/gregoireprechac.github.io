Coeur de Crissier

A new mixed-use building complex at Route du Bois-Genoud 2, Crissier, opening Q4 2027. The project is creating a startup-ready ecosystem for companies in health, science, and technology.

Building a startup ecosystem
in the Lausanne region.

Coeur de Crissier is a 7,000 m² Minergie-certified complex in Crissier, VD, designed for companies in health, science, and technology. The building offers modular spaces from 500 m², including laboratories, showrooms, R&D areas, and offices, all available as raw shell spaces configured to each tenant's needs. Delivery is planned for Q4 2027.

The project sits at a strategic location in the western Lausanne area, close to EPFL and UNIL, and within the canton's main innovation corridor. The ambition goes beyond real estate: the goal is to position the building as a node in the Vaud startup ecosystem, connecting it to the university research pipeline and the cantonal institutions that support innovation and economic development.

The work involves mapping the startup and scaleup landscape of the canton, identifying companies whose spatial and operational needs match the building's profile, and building relationships with the key actors in the regional innovation ecosystem.
